Reliable Mini Excavation for Muskoka Properties

Hammel Bay Homes provides professional mini excavation services throughout the Muskoka region. Based in Utterson, we specialize in residential and cottage property excavation projects using a Bobcat 5.5-ton mini excavator.

Our equipment is ideal for trenching, drainage improvements, driveway preparation and projects where larger excavation equipment cannot easily access the property.
