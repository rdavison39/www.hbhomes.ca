Excavation Services Across Muskoka

Hammel Bay Homes provides excavation and precision rock blasting services across the Muskoka region. Based in Utterson, we specialize in projects for cottages, residential properties and small construction jobs.

Our mini excavator combined with NxBurst technology allows us to work efficiently in tight spaces while safely handling rock removal where traditional methods are not practical.

Why Use a Mini Excavator?

Mini excavators are ideal for Muskoka properties where access may be limited by trees, terrain or existing buildings. They allow precise excavation with minimal disturbance.

Combined with controlled rock breaking, we can handle excavation projects that would otherwise require larger equipment or traditional blasting.
