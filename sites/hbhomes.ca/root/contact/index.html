Mini Excavation Services Across Muskoka

Hammel Bay Homes provides mini excavation services across the Muskoka region including Huntsville, Bracebridge, Lake of Bays and Gravenhurst. Our mini excavator is ideal for cottage properties where space is limited and larger excavation equipment cannot easily access the job site.

Common excavation projects in Muskoka include trenching for water lines and hydro conduit, driveway excavation, drainage improvements around cottages and culvert installation for private roads. We work with homeowners and contractors to complete excavation projects safely and efficiently.
