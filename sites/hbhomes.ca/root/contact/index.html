Excavation & Rock Blasting Services Across Muskoka

Hammel Bay Homes provides excavation and precision rock blasting services across the Muskoka region including Huntsville, Bracebridge, Lake of Bays and Gravenhurst. Our equipment and NxBurst technology allow us to work efficiently in tight-access cottage environments where traditional methods are not practical.

Common projects include trenching for water lines and hydro conduit, driveway excavation, drainage improvements, foundation preparation, and controlled rock breaking where bedrock is present.
